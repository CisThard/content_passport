### 상금 구조

Sui Overflow 2026의 상금은 해커톤 이후 지속적인 개발을 장려하기 위해 **분할 배분 모델**을 따릅니다:

- **상금의 50%**는 수상자 발표 시 지급
- **상금의 50%**는 메인넷 성공 배포 후 지급
- 수상 팀이 수상자 발표(8월) 시점에 이미 프로젝트를 메인넷에 배포한 경우, **상금 100%를 선지급**받습니다.

**유의사항:**
- 메인넷 배포는 Sui 팀 및/또는 트랙 스폰서가 정의한 최소 기능 요건을 충족해야 합니다.
- 팀은 해커톤 이후 지속적으로 빌딩하여 상금 전액을 확보할 것을 권장합니다.

### Prize Structure

Sui Overflow 2026 prizes follow a **split disbursement model** to encourage continued development post-hackathon:

- **50% of prize** paid at time of winners announcement
- **50% of prize** paid upon successful mainnet deployment
- If a winning team has already deployed to mainnet by the time of winners announcement (August), they receive **100% of their prize upfront**

**Important Notes:**
- Mainnet deployment must meet minimum feature requirements defined by the Sui team and/or track sponsors.
- Teams are encouraged to continue building post-hackathon to secure the full prize.

### Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

**Q: What are the eligibility requirements for Overflow?**
A: Projects must be deployed on Sui mainnet or testnet by Demo Day and shortlisting. Submissions must be either completely new projects built between May 7 and June 21, 2026, or existing Sui projects with substantial new features developed during the same period.

**Q: What if my project spans multiple tracks?**
A: Each submission can only be submitted to one primary track. Teams should choose the track that best represents the project's core focus and value proposition.

**Q: Can I use AI-assisted tools?**
A: Yes. AI-assisted development tools are allowed and expected. Projects are evaluated on product quality, technical execution, creativity, usability, and practical value — not the amount of manually written code.

**Q: Do I need to complete KYC?**
A: KYC is generally only required if your project wins a prize. At least one member of the winning team must successfully complete KYC to receive payment.
